# Pet care planning (non-medical)

This handbook supports PawPal+ scheduling tips only. It is **not** veterinary advice.

## Daily routines

Consistency reduces stress for pets. Anchor feeding, walks, and medication reminders to the same windows when possible. Shorter, predictable sessions beat occasional marathons.

## Time budgeting

If total care tasks exceed the minutes you have today, raise priority on safety-critical items (feeding, prescribed meds administration scheduling—but never dosing advice), then enrichment, then optional grooming.

## Multi-pet homes

Interleave species-specific tasks when possible to avoid bottlenecks. Label tasks per pet so schedules stay auditable.

## When to call a professional

Sudden behavior changes, refusal to eat, difficulty breathing, repeated vomiting, or suspected toxin exposure require a **licensed veterinarian**, not an app.
